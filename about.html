The Story

Misaki Zuno 造

Where craftsmanship meets intelligence — two decades of global footwear manufacturing, distilled into actionable industry insight.

I have spent over 20 years inside the global footwear manufacturing ecosystem — not observing it from the outside, but embedded within it. From pattern rooms in Guangzhou to last development meetings in Ho Chi Minh City, from tannery visits in Santa Croce to costing negotiations in Dhaka.

What I found was a gap: the people making decisions — sourcing managers, brand executives, factory directors — were often the last to see the signals that mattered. Industry news fragmented across dozens of trade publications, buried in paywalls, or arriving too late to act on. I built iamshoemaker.com to close that gap.

This is not a media company. It is an intelligence operation, run by someone who has stood on the factory floor and understands what a 2% FOB shift actually means for a production cycle, what a quality recall really costs upstream, and where the next manufacturing geography is quietly being built — before the brands announce it.

Quality & Standards

From inline QC process design to SATRA compliance, material testing protocols, and failure mode analysis. Two decades of understanding where product quality actually breaks down — and why.

  • SATRA and ISO testing standards

Brand Strategy
